I. Spring Clothing (Pure Cotton, Light Denim, Knit Cotton, Lightweight Synthetic)

1. Pure Cotton (Spring Shirts, T-shirts, Casual Pants)

Washing: Machine washable or hand washable, water temperature ≤30°C, neutral detergent. Wash lights and darks separately. Add a small amount of salt during the first wash to set colors. Use a laundry bag + gentle cycle for machine washing to avoid pilling from friction.

Care: Air-dry in a cool, ventilated area; avoid direct sunlight to prevent fading. Ensure completely dry before storage; fold or hang on wide-shoulder hangers. Place desiccants in humid weather to prevent mold.

2. Light Denim (Spring Jeans, Denim Jackets)

Washing: Water temperature ≤30°C, neutral detergent. Turn inside out to reduce fading; select gentle cycle for machine washing. Avoid frequent washing — once every 1-2 weeks is sufficient.

Care: Turn inside out and air-dry in shade; avoid direct sunlight. Store by folding flat or hanging; use wide-shoulder hangers to prevent waistband distortion. Place moisture-proof paper in pockets to maintain shape.

3. Knit Cotton (Spring Knit Sweaters, Light Cardigans)

Washing: Hand wash preferred, water temperature ≤25°C, gently press with neutral detergent. For machine washing, use a laundry bag and select knit-specific cycle. Do not rub vigorously or wring.

Care: Lay flat to air-dry; avoid hanging to prevent collar stretching. Fold for storage; add mothballs for moth prevention. Use a fabric shaver to remove minor pilling.

4. Lightweight Synthetic (Spring Trench Coats, Sun-Protection Jackets)

Washing: Machine washable, water temperature 30-40°C, neutral or alkaline detergent. Gently rub; lightly brush stubborn stains. Add fabric softener during washing to reduce static.

Care: Can be sun-dried; turn over promptly to ensure thorough drying. Can be folded or hung for storage; avoid heavy pressure that causes permanent creasing.

II. Summer Clothing (Silk, Cotton-Linen, Ice Silk, Short-Sleeve Cotton, Chiffon)

1. Silk (Summer Dresses, Blouses)

Washing: Dry cleaning recommended. For hand washing, use silk-specific neutral detergent, water temperature ≤25°C, soak ≤15 minutes, gently press to wash. Do not rub, wring, or bleach.

Care: Air-dry in a cool, ventilated area; avoid direct sunlight. Use smooth hangers to prevent snagging; fold for storage and avoid heavy pressure. Iron with low-temperature steam, keeping 1-2cm distance from the fabric.

2. Cotton-Linen (Summer T-shirts, Wide-Leg Pants, Shirts)

Washing: Machine washable or hand washable, water temperature ≤30°C, neutral detergent. Cotton-linen wrinkles easily — no need to rub hard. Wash lights and darks separately.

Care: Can be sun-dried; promptly store after drying. Iron smooth before storage; can be folded or hung. Avoid humid environments to prevent mold.

3. Ice Silk (Summer T-shirts, Shorts, Dresses)

Washing: Hand wash or machine wash, water temperature ≤30°C, neutral detergent. Use a laundry bag for machine washing; select gentle cycle. Do not soak for extended periods (≤10 minutes).

Care: Air-dry in shade; avoid direct sunlight and high-temperature drying. Fold flat for storage; avoid sharp objects that could snag the fabric. Avoid friction with rough objects when wearing.

4. Chiffon (Summer Dresses, Sun-Protection Cardigans)

Washing: Hand wash preferred, water temperature ≤30°C, gently rinse with neutral detergent. Use a laundry bag for machine washing; select gentle cycle. Do not wring forcefully.

Care: Air-dry in shade; use thin hangers when hanging to avoid snagging. Place a thin layer of tissue paper between folds to prevent sticking. Use low-temperature steam ironing for minor wrinkles.

III. Autumn Clothing (Wool, Cashmere, Heavy Denim, Corduroy, Suede)

1. Wool/Cashmere (Autumn Wool Sweaters, Light Cashmere Coats)

Washing: Dry cleaning preferred. For hand washing, use wool-specific detergent, water temperature ≤20°C, soak ≤15 minutes, gently press. Do not machine wash, rub, or wring.

Care: Lay flat to air-dry; avoid hanging to prevent stretching. Store with moth repellents (mothballs, lavender sachets), wrap in breathable fabric bags. Use wide-shoulder hangers or fold for storage; avoid heavy pressure.

2. Heavy Denim (Autumn Denim Jackets, Heavy Jeans)

Washing: Water temperature ≤30°C, neutral detergent. Turn inside out to reduce fading and wear; select standard cycle for machine washing. Soak in salt water for 30 minutes during the first wash to set colors.

Care: Turn inside out and air-dry in shade; avoid direct sunlight. Store by folding flat or hanging on wide-shoulder hangers. Ensure completely dry before long-term storage; add moisture-proof agents.

3. Corduroy (Autumn Corduroy Jackets, Pants)

Washing: Hand wash or machine wash, water temperature ≤30°C, neutral detergent. Use a laundry bag for machine washing; select gentle cycle. Do not rub vigorously to avoid disturbing the pile surface.

Care: Air-dry in shade with the reverse side facing up. Fold for storage; avoid heavy pressure that damages the pile surface. Iron with low-temperature steam, placing a thin cloth between the iron and fabric, ironing in the direction of the pile.

4. Suede (Autumn Suede Jackets)

Washing: Dry cleaning recommended. Faux suede can be hand washed, water temperature ≤30°C, gently press with neutral detergent. Do not machine wash, bleach, or wring forcefully.

Care: Air-dry in shade; avoid direct sunlight and high temperatures. Hang for storage; avoid folding to prevent creases. Gently brush surface dust with a soft-bristle brush.

IV. Winter Clothing (Down Jackets, Heavy Wool Coats, Fleece-Lined Denim, Thermal Underwear)

1. Down (Winter Down Jackets, Down Vests)

Washing: Dry cleaning preferred. For washable styles, use down-specific detergent, water temperature ≤30°C, soak ≤20 minutes, gently press. Select down-specific cycle for machine washing; use a laundry bag.

Care: Air-dry in a ventilated, cool area; gently pat during drying to restore fluffiness. Ensure completely dry before storage; do not over-compress in vacuum bags. Periodically take out, pat, and air.

2. Heavy Wool Coat (Winter Wool Coats, Wool-Blend Outerwear)

Washing: Must be dry cleaned — dry cleaning preserves the elasticity and softness of wool fibers. Do not hand wash or machine wash to avoid fiber felting and shrinkage.

Care: Hang using wide-shoulder seamless hangers, away from moisture and heat. Pat to remove dust before storage; add moth repellents. Avoid contact with sharp objects.

3. Fleece-Lined Denim (Winter Fleece-Lined Jeans, Fleece-Lined Denim Jackets)

Washing: Water temperature ≤30°C, neutral detergent. Turn inside out for washing; select gentle cycle for machine washing. Avoid extended soaking to reduce fleece shedding.

Care: Turn inside out and air-dry in shade; avoid direct sunlight. Fold flat for storage; avoid heavy pressure that damages the fleece lining. Dry promptly after washing to prevent mold and odors.

4. Thermal Underwear (Cotton Thermal Underwear, German Velvet Thermal Underwear)

Washing: Machine washable or hand washable, water temperature ≤30°C, neutral detergent. Select gentle cycle for machine washing; avoid strong spin cycles. Do not use bleach.

Care: Air-dry in shade or briefly sun-dry. Fold neatly and store in a dry place. Avoid high-temperature ironing for German velvet materials to prevent damaging thermal fibers.

V. General Care Tips

1. Wash different materials separately to avoid color transfer and abrasion. Fully dissolve detergent before adding clothes to avoid localized discoloration.

2. Treat stains promptly — the longer you wait, the harder they are to remove. Treat different stains specifically (use dish soap concentrate for oil stains; soak blood stains in cold water).

3. Check care labels before ironing and adjust temperature according to material. Test iron on the inside of the garment first to avoid scorching.

4. For long-term storage, ensure garments are completely dry before storing. Periodically check for mold and moth damage.
