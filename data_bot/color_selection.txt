1. Skin Tone & Clothing Color Matching Principles
   Cool White Skin: Suits both cool and warm tones. Bright colors (such as royal blue, true red, mint green) enhance a fair complexion; dark colors (such as black, dark gray) add presence while avoiding a washed-out look.
   Yellow/Warm Yellow Skin: Prioritize warm tones (such as caramel, ginger yellow, dusty rose). Avoid cool fluorescent colors (such as neon green, cool pink), which can make the complexion look dull. Light beige and oatmeal tones soften skin tone and brighten the complexion.
   Dark Skin: Suits high-saturation bright colors (such as bright yellow, orange, lake blue) to highlight a healthy complexion. Avoid dull olive-brown and gray-brown tones, which can make the complexion look lackluster.

2. Occasion-Based Clothing Color Selection
   Daily Commute: Stick to basic colors (black, white, gray, beige, navy) for a clean, polished look. Add low-saturation bright accents (such as haze blue, lavender) for a touch of energy.
   Formal Occasions (Business Meetings/Interviews): Opt for dark tones (black, navy, dark gray) for a professional, composed appearance. Avoid large areas of bright colors and flashy patterns; keep it simple and appropriate.
   Casual Occasions (Shopping/Outings): Choose high-saturation colors or contrasting color combinations (such as yellow + white, blue + white) for a fresh, lively look. Stripes and plaid patterns also work well for casual settings.
   Banquets/Parties: Go for bright colors (true red, wine red, royal blue) or metallic tones (gold, silver) to make a statement. Avoid overly plain colors that diminish your presence.

3. Body Type & Clothing Color Styling Tips
   Slimming Outfits: Prioritize dark tones (black, dark gray, navy) to visually narrow the silhouette. Avoid large areas of bright colors and horizontal stripes, which can add visual bulk.
   Taller Appearance: Light top, dark bottom (e.g., light pink top + black pants) creates a visual contrast that elongates proportions. Monochrome outfits (e.g., all beige, all black) create a streamlined look.
   Fuller Body Types: Avoid tight-fitting + bright color combinations. Choose matte-finish dark clothing and use small bright accessories (such as scarves, necklaces) to brighten the look.
   Petite Body Types: Lighter and warmer tones (such as white, beige, light yellow) visually add fullness. Avoid oversized dark clothing, which can make a small frame appear even more slight.

4. Seasonal Clothing Color Selection
   Spring: Fresh, soft colors (such as cherry blossom pink, mint green, light blue) match the renewal of the season.
   Summer: Cool tones and light colors (such as white, sky blue, light green) feel refreshing and cooling. Avoid dark tones, which absorb heat and look heavy.
   Autumn: Warm tones and earth tones (such as caramel, khaki, brown) echo the season of falling leaves and harvest.
   Winter: Dark tones and high-saturation bright colors (such as black, wine red, dark green) provide warmth and presence. A white down jacket is also a winter classic — clean and sharp.

5. Color Matching Taboos
   Avoid large-area high-saturation color clashes (such as red + green, blue + orange), which look chaotic and jarring. Use neutral tones (black, white, gray) as transitions.
   Yellow skin tones should avoid cool-toned pinks and purples, which can make the complexion look sallow. Dark skin tones should avoid fluorescent colors, which can make the complexion look dull.
   For formal occasions, avoid combining more than three colors — keep it simple and coordinated. For casual occasions, you can add color layers, but maintain a primary-secondary hierarchy.

6. Accessory Color Matching Tips
   Monochrome Accessories: Match the clothing color (e.g., black top + black bag) for a minimalist, high-end look — ideal for commuting.
   Contrasting Accessories: Create a contrast with the clothing color (e.g., white dress + red necklace) to brighten the outfit — suitable for casual and party settings.
   Neutral Accessories: Black, white, gray, and metallic accessories are universally versatile and pair with any clothing color — the safe choice for any occasion.
